Coconut Maturity

Detection System

The Coconut Maturity Detection System is an AI-powered tool designed to help farmers and agricultural professionals accurately determine the ripeness of coconuts using images. By analyzing visual characteristics, the system can classify coconuts as unripe, mature, or overripe, making harvest timing more efficient and reducing crop waste. With its user-friendly interface and fast results, this technology brings modern innovation to coconut farming and helps boost productivity in the field.

About

Our Coconut Maturity Detection System uses machine learning to analyze coconut images captured with a smartphone. By recognizing patterns in color, texture, and shape, the system can accurately classify coconuts as unripe, mature, or overripe. This helps farmers make better harvesting decisions, reduce waste, and improve crop quality. With a user-friendly interface and the power of AI, coconut farming becomes smarter, faster, and more efficient.
